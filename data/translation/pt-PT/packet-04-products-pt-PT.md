## Scope
- Product titles
- Product SEO titles
- Product SEO meta descriptions
- Product body descriptions

## Translation notes
- Product and fragrance names are localized into pt-PT for storefront clarity after explicit user approval; keep SKU/search handles in English.
- Translate category/function copy around the product name into pt-PT.
- Keep AROMA + WAX, REACH, IFRA, SDS, CLP, UFI, OEKO-TEX, RAL-GZ 041, LVP-VOC and wax melts unchanged where used as recognised labels/terms.
- Use pt-PT terminology from `TERMINOLOGY_RESEARCH_PT_PT.md`: `fragrâncias`, `óleos de fragrância`, `difusor de varetas`, `spray de ambiente`, `fabrico de velas`, `pavios`, `ceras`, `corantes`, `varetas`, `base para difusor` and `descontos por volume`.
