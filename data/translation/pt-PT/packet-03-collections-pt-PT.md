- Collection SEO titles

## Translation notes
- Keep AROMA + WAX as the brand name.
- Keep `wax melts`, REACH, IFRA, SDS, CLP and UFI unchanged where used as labels or recognised terms.
- Product and fragrance names are localized into pt-PT for storefront copy after explicit approval; keep handles/SKUs in English.
- Use pt-PT terminology from `TERMINOLOGY_RESEARCH_PT_PT.md`: `fragrâncias`, `óleos de fragrância`, `difusor de varetas`, `spray de ambiente`, `fabrico de velas`, `pavios`, `ceras`, `corantes`, `venda grossista` and `marca própria`.
- Portuguese is not published by this packet. This is a local editorial draft only.
